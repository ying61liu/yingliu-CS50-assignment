This program in C simulates a runoff election. 
In an instant runoff election, voters can rank as many candidates as they wish. 
If an candidate has a majority (more than 50%) of the first preference votes, that candidate is declared the winnner of the election.
If no candidate has more than 50% of the vote, then an "instant runoff" occurs. 
The candidate who received the fewest number of votes is eliminated from the election, and anyone who originally chose that candidate
as their first preference now has their second preference considered. 
The process repeats. Once a candidate has a majority, that candidate is declared the winner.
